Game : the game itself
Player : the character controlled by the user
Level : a square containing a village, a dungeon, a river, a bridge, and in wich the player
	can walk
World : the 15 levels generated int he game
Village : the starting position of every level
Dungeon : the exit position of every level
Dirt : just floor
River : a non walkable part of the level
Bridge : 3 tiles that the player can walk on to get on the other side of the river
